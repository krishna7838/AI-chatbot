BTech (Bachelor of Technology) is a four-year undergraduate engineering program offered by universities and technical institutes in India and abroad. It is designed to provide theoretical knowledge and practical training in various engineering disciplines.

Eligibility:
- Students must complete their 10+2 education (PCM stream: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics).
- Admission is based on entrance exams such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced, or state-level exams (e.g., KCET, MHT-CET).

Duration:
- The course is typically divided into 8 semesters over 4 years.
- It includes classroom lectures, lab sessions, minor and major projects, internships, and seminars.

Common Specializations:
- Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Information Technology (IT)
- Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Mechanical Engineering (ME)
- Civil Engineering (CE)
- Electrical Engineering (EE)
- Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
- Biotechnology
- Chemical Engineering

Core Subjects (vary by specialization):
- Mathematics, Physics, Engineering Mechanics
- Programming in C/C++, Python
- Data Structures and Algorithms
- Database Management Systems
- Operating Systems
- Computer Networks
- Machine Learning and AI
- Thermodynamics
- Fluid Mechanics
- Structural Engineering
- Digital Electronics

Internships and Projects:
- Many colleges include industry internships during the 6th or 7th semester.
- Final year students typically undertake major projects or research work.

Career Opportunities:
- Software Engineer, Data Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer
- Civil Engineer, Site Supervisor, Design Engineer
- Hardware Engineer, Network Engineer
- Government exams like GATE, ESE, PSU recruitment
- Higher studies like MTech, MBA, or studying abroad

Skills Developed:
- Analytical and problem-solving skills
- Programming and technical proficiency
- Communication and teamwork
- Project management

Top Recruiters:
- TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Google, Amazon, Microsoft, L&T, Tata Steel, ONGC, ISRO

Higher Study Options:
- MTech (Master of Technology)
- MS in USA/Germany/Canada
- MBA (Technology Management, Business Analytics, etc.)
- Certifications in Data Science, AI, Cloud, etc.

BTech is considered one of the most valuable and versatile degrees for students aiming to build a career in the technical, engineering, or IT domain.
